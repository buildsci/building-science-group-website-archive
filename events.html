Dr. Farber speaks in UMD ME Seminar Series

12/10/2014: Dr. Darryl Farber, an Assistant Professor in the Pennsylvania State University's College of Engineering, spoke as part of the University of Maryland's Department of Mechanical Engineering 2014 Seminar Series. Attended by members of the Cluster for Sustainbaility and UMD ME students, he spoke about the "Strategic Analysis of Interdependent Infrastructures." Dr. Jelena Srebric hosted Dr. Farber as the seminar speaker.

CITY @ UMD Members meet with UMD Facility Management Officials

11/26/2014: Members of the Cluster for Sustainability in the Built Environment at the Univeristy of Maryland met with officials from the University of Maryland's Facilities Managment. They discussed the University's utility metering system, energy & infrastructure goals, and future collaborations between Facility Management and the members of CITY@UMD.

Presentation | Real Time University

CITY @ UMD Members meet with USGBC Research Officials

11/13/2014: Dr. Srebric, Dr. Heidarinejad, Matt Dahlhausen, Nicholas Mattise, and Saber Koshdel-Nikkho met with research leaders of the US Green Building Council. Previously Building Science Group members co-authored a paper on the cluster of simulated energy use of LEED certified buildings. Current research and future potential collaborations were discussed.

Heidarinejad and Mattise Present at ASHRAE/IBPSA Building Simulation 2014

09/10 - 09/12/2014: Dr. Mohammad Heidarinejad and Nicholas Mattise, both of the Mechanical Engineering Department of the University of Maryland, present a paper at the 2014 ASHRAE/IBPSA Building SimulationConference in Atlanta, GA. The paper, on the online integration of energy, daylight, and airflow simulations, covered work done on the EEB Hub Simulation Platform Project.

Presentated Paper | EEB Hub Simulation Project

Mohammad Heidarinejad Recieves Travel Awards

09/10/2014: Dr. Mohammad Heidarinejad, a postdoctoral research associate in the Mechanical Engineering Department at The University of Maryland was awarded travel awards to the 2014 ASHRAE Building Simulation Conference (provided by IBPSA USA) and one to the Symposium on Building Energy Uncertainty and Risk Analysis (a NSF Sponsored Workshop). Both events are to be held the week of September 8, 2014 in Atlanta, GA.
